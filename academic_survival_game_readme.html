THE LORE
You are a 4th year student trapped inside a sprawling academic map with one goal — survive until graduation. Carve out free time by encircling it with movement, but beware: the campus is infested with academic threats that grow larger and faster as the semester drags on. One wrong move and your trail is broken. Your attendance is gone. Your GPA is dust.

You have only your wits, a hall ticket, and two miracle power-ups standing between you and academic oblivion.
🕹
CORE MECHANICS
🧍
Player Movement
Move freely across the academic map. Leave a glowing trail wherever you go — your projected ambitions.
⬛
Area Capture
Complete a closed loop to claim territory. The bigger the loop, the higher the reward — and the higher the risk.
💀
Instant Game Over
Enemy touches your trail or your body = GAME OVER. No second chances. Just like real college.
📊
Score = Area %
Score is based on total map area captured. Hit 75%+ to pass the semester with dignity.

FINAL BOSS
🎓
THE HOD
He has seen empires fall. Bigger than the ESE, faster than your excuses, and completely immune to your sob story. The HOD patrols every corner of the map simultaneously. He knows where your trail leads before you do. One brush of his presence and your enrollment is cancelled.

To survive, you must capture over 80% of the map while he hunts you. No power-ups. No checkpoints. Just you and the HOD.
SIZE: MASSIVE SPEED: LUDICROUS MERCY: NULL IMMUNE TO EXCUSES
⚡
POWER-UPS
POWER-UP 01
Proxy Attendance
Your trusted ally answers for you. For a limited time, enemies believe you're somewhere else entirely — phasing through your trail without triggering a game over. Works once per enemy wave. Best deployed mid-capture when an MSE or ESE cuts off your escape route.
DURATION: 8 SEC CHARGES: 2/GAME RISK: MEDIUM
POWER-UP 02
Mass Bunker
You and every student on the map enters complete lockdown. All enemies freeze in place — unable to process paperwork, let alone hunt you. Claim a massive area risk-free. Cannot be used during the HOD boss fight because the HOD signed the bunker order.
DURATION: 12 SEC CHARGES: 1/GAME RISK: NONE

SURVIVAL TIPS
  • ▶Start with small captures near the walls — short trails mean shorter exposure windows.
  • ▶Never capture big territory when an ESE or HOD is nearby. Patience keeps your CGPA alive.
  • ▶Save Mass Bunker for the ESE wave — it's the one moment where a massive land grab is actually viable.
  • ▶Proxy Attendance is a trail shield, not an invisibility cloak. Enemies can still collide with your body.
  • ▶The HOD moves toward your trail endpoint. Bait him into a corner, then loop back from the opposite side.
  • ▶75% map capture unlocks a passing grade. Anything above that is for the brave — or the very foolish.
